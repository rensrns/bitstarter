At OVERBOUGHT.COM we merge a national 'reuse' site with online shopping. Shop at Overbought for potential savings and at the same time help decrease the strain on our landfills. An inventory of most homes will uncover items for which there is no longer a need nor storage; books, parts from old appliances, car parts for vehicles no longer owned (belts, oil/air filters), sports equipment no longer used, bags of gizmos from the box store down the street (you couldn't buy just the one you needed), or maybe you bought the pack of six because it was almost as cheap as the pack of two. Overbought members can de-clutter, earn shipping credits, and save money. Links to online retailers are included to provide you with search options and to assist you in acquiring what you need.

Become an Overbought member today. When you wish to offer an item for reuse just enter a short description, the items dimensions and weight, and any available id/serial numbers. When possible, help your fellow members by uploading a picture of the offered item. If there is a request for your offer, we will make the transaction as easy and as anonymous as possible. The requester pays for all postage and handling. Just insert the item into the pre-paid, labeled container delivered to your residence. Then return the container to your mail carrier. For each successful item shared you earn a credit that can be used to offset mailing costs on the next item that you find in our database.

FAQ

Is there a charge to be a share member?
There is no charge to be a share member. Site maintenace is dependent upon advertising revenue.
If I have large or otherwise un-mailable items can I restrict my offer to a region local to my residence?
Yes, users will be able choose between local, regional, and national audiences.
